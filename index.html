The Initiative

About LEX Briefly

LEX Briefly is a student-driven initiative created to simplify legal education for students of the Faculty of Law, University of Delhi.

We provide organized and accessible legal resources that help students excel academically and professionally. Navigating massive syllabi, scattered case mats, and finding reliable notes can be overwhelming. We solve that by centralizing everything.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and practical legal knowledge through quality study materials, case law resources, legal updates, and career opportunities—accessible to everyone, anytime.
